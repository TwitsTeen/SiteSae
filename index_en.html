About us

Founded in 2023, Universus is a company that aims towards creating sports events between french colleges.
Notre objectif est de permettre aux étudiants le souhaitant de se dépenser, de représenter leur université à l’échelle nationale, mais aussi d’organiser un événement agréable pour les spectateurs, et d’offrir un spectacle de qualité.
Cette envie nous vient de notre propre cursus, et de nos envies de faire du sport universitaire compétitif, et en ayant constaté un manque criant d’opportunités, nous avons décidé de retrousser nos manches, et de nous en occuper personnellement.

What are we offering?

Universus is based on 6 main sports : football (soccer for fellow USA citizens), basketball, handball, rugby, tennis and badminton. Multiple events with these sports are staged all around France, and are offered for each and every college

Where can we be found? ?

Our long term objective is to allow every single french college to clash against each other, on the basis of a sufficient organization. However, we did not reach this point quite yet, and agreed on a partnership with a number of colleges from the start :

colleges from Lille, Arras, Toulouse, Avignon, Grenoble, Limoges, Le Mans, Orléans, Brest, Caen, Le Havre, Dijon and Nancy.

Competition, easier.
